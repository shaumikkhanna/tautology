How to Play?

🎲 Objective

Farkle Frenzy is a push-your-luck dice game. Your goal is to be the first player to reach 10,000 points by rolling combinations that score. After a player crosses 10,000, all other players get one last chance to beat that score.

🎯 On Your Turn

You start your turn by rolling six dice. After each roll, you must lock in at least one scoring die before choosing to roll again or stop and bank your points.

You may roll up to three times in a single turn, but each time you risk getting a Farkle—a roll with no scoring dice—which causes you to lose all points for that turn.

🔥 Hot Dice

If you manage to score with all six dice in a single turn, you get Hot Dice. Your points are banked automatically and you immediately get a new set of six dice to continue your turn.

💥 Farkle

If none of your rolled dice score, you Farkle. You lose all points accumulated during that turn, and your turn ends immediately.

💾 Banking Points

At any point after locking in scoring dice, you may choose to stop and bank your points from the current turn. These are then added to your total score.

🏆 Winning the Game

Once a player crosses 10,000 points, all others get a final turn. At the end of that round, the player with the highest score wins!
